About

Who we are

Founded in 2025, GlobalEd Consulting helps students achieve their international education goals through professional guidance, personalized support, and direct access to trusted global opportunities.

We work closely with universities, educational institutions, and international organizations to provide reliable and up-to-date pathways for students worldwide. Our mission is to help every student choose the right major, university, and academic direction based on their goals, interests, and future career plans.

With a professional and experienced team, we offer comprehensive consultation across university admissions, scholarships, visa preparation, and student support. We believe every student deserves a unique strategy, and we are committed to making the admission journey clearer, easier, and more successful.

At GlobalEd Consulting, we do more than process applications — we help students build a brighter future through quality international education and meaningful global opportunities.
